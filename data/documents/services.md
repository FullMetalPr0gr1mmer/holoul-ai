# Holoul Services

Holoul provides four core services covering the full e-waste lifecycle.

## 1. E-waste & IT Asset Recycling

A three-step treatment process:

1. **Segregation** — incoming material is sorted by category (IT equipment,
   consumer electronics, storage media, components, batteries).
2. **Hazardous removal** — hazardous items such as batteries and mercury tubes
   are removed and handled through dedicated hazardous streams before mechanical
   processing.
3. **Automated shredding** — the remaining material is passed through Holoul's
   custom-built automatic shredding plant, and recovered fractions (ferrous and
   non-ferrous metals, plastics, circuit-board material) are baled for
   downstream recovery.

## 2. Mobile Secure Data Destruction

Holoul can destroy data-bearing media at the customer's own site. On-site
destruction is documented with CCTV recording, and a destruction certificate is
issued for every job. Video verification is available on request. Methods
include degaussing, physical destruction, and shredding.

## 3. Product Disposal

Discreet, brand-protective disposal of surplus, obsolete, or defective inventory.
This service prevents grey-market resale of recalled or unsold products while
recovering material value.

## 4. Packaging & Transportation

Holoul supplies collection containers sized for any volume — from small bins,
cages, and stillages up to large bulk-loader bins — and handles transportation
from the customer's premises to the appropriate treatment facility.

## Turnaround and reporting

Each collection is logged as a pickup with a weight and container type; customers
receive processing status updates (Scheduled → Collected → Processing →
Completed) and, where applicable, a recovery report and invoice.
