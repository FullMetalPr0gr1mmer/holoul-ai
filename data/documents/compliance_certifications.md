# Compliance & Certifications

Holoul operates to internationally recognised environmental, safety, and
electronics-recycling standards.

## Certifications and standards

- **ISO 14001** — Environmental Management System. Governs how Holoul identifies
  and controls the environmental impact of its treatment operations.
- **OHSAS 18001** — Occupational Health and Safety Management. Covers worker
  safety across collection, transport, and processing.
- **R2:2013 (Responsible Recycling)** — the electronics-recycling industry
  standard for responsible reuse, recovery, and disposal, including data security
  and downstream due diligence.
- **Basel Convention** — Holoul operates in line with the Basel Convention on the
  control of transboundary movements of hazardous wastes.

## Traceability and downstream due diligence

Holoul's tracking system records material from the point of receipt through
processing to the downstream partner that receives each recovered fraction. This
supports R2 downstream due-diligence requirements and gives customers an
auditable environmental trail for their disposed assets.

## Facility controls

All facilities operate with CCTV surveillance, metal detection at entry/exit, and
air-filtration on the shredding line to control particulate emissions.
