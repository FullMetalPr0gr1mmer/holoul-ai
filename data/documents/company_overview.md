# Holoul Electronic Recycling — Company Overview

Holoul Electronic Recycling Treatment Company is an electronic-waste (e-waste)
recycling enterprise operating across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the wider
MENA region. Established in 2012 as a joint venture between Tadweer, Saker Al
Jazirah, and Rotoub, Holoul was the first company of its kind in the Kingdom to
provide end-to-end treatment of Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment (WEEE).

## Mission

Holoul's mission is to keep hazardous and valuable electronic waste out of
landfill by recovering reusable materials, safely destroying data-bearing
devices, and returning recovered commodities (metals, plastics, precious metals)
to the circular economy through certified downstream partners.

## Facilities

Holoul operates purpose-built treatment plants:

- **Jeddah Plant** — Building 6237, Al Mesk Street, Jeddah 3rd Industrial Area.
  Holoul's largest facility, housing a custom-built automatic shredding line with
  air-filtration and a full material-tracking system.
- **Riyadh Plant** — located in the Riyadh 2nd Industrial City, serving central
  region customers.
- **Dammam Hub** — a collection and consolidation hub serving the Eastern
  Province.

All facilities are protected by CCTV surveillance and metal detection, and
provide full material traceability from the point of receipt through to
downstream recycling partners.

## Who we serve

Holoul serves local government agencies, telecommunications operators, banks and
financial institutions, healthcare providers, technology firms, and individual
consumers. Because these customers often dispose of data-bearing assets, secure
data destruction and chain-of-custody documentation are central to Holoul's
service offering.
