# Accepted and Non-Accepted Materials

## Materials Holoul accepts

Holoul accepts a broad range of electrical and electronic equipment, including:

- **IT equipment**: laptops, desktops, servers, networking gear.
- **Consumer electronics**: monitors, televisions, mobile phones.
- **Storage media**: hard drives, solid-state drives, tapes and cartridges,
  flash memory, DVDs and CDs.
- **Components**: circuit boards, cables and wiring, power supplies, toner and
  ink cartridges.
- **Batteries and lamps**: rechargeable batteries and mercury tubes (handled as
  hazardous material).

Household electronic goods are also accepted from consumers.

## Materials Holoul does NOT accept

Holoul does not accept large "white goods" and refrigerant-containing
appliances, specifically:

- Washing machines
- Tumble dryers
- Refrigerators and freezers
- Air-conditioning units

These items require specialised refrigerant-handling and are outside Holoul's
WEEE treatment scope.

## Hazardous handling

Hazardous items — chiefly batteries, mercury tubes, and toner/ink — are removed
during segregation and processed through dedicated hazardous-waste streams rather
than the general shredding line.
