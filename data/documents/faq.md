# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I arrange a collection?

Contact Holoul to schedule a pickup. Holoul will recommend a container type based
on your volume — small bins and cages for offices, stillages and bulk-loader bins
for large clearances — and arrange transport to the nearest facility.

## What container types are available?

Small Bin, Cage, Stillage, and Bulk Loader Bin. The right choice depends on the
volume and type of equipment being disposed of.

## Do you provide a certificate for data destruction?

Yes. Every secure data destruction job is issued a unique destruction certificate
recording the number of devices, the method used (degaussing, shredding, or
physical destruction), and the date. The event is recorded on CCTV and video
verification is available on request.

## Which regions do you serve?

Holoul serves customers across Saudi Arabia, with treatment plants in Jeddah and
Riyadh and a consolidation hub in Dammam, plus collection across the wider MENA
region.

## What happens to the material after processing?

Recovered fractions — metals, plastics, and circuit-board material — are baled and
shipped to certified downstream recycling partners. Holoul's tracking system
maintains full traceability from receipt to downstream partner.

## Is there value recovered from my e-waste?

Many materials carry recovery value — circuit boards, mobile phones, and SSDs are
among the highest-value fractions per kilogram, while bulk items such as monitors
and televisions carry lower per-kg value. Holoul can report recovered value where
applicable.

## Do you handle hazardous items?

Yes — batteries, mercury tubes, and toner/ink are removed during segregation and
processed through dedicated hazardous-waste streams in line with Holoul's
environmental permits.
